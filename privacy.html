Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

At NodnWebTools, accessible from our domain, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by NodnWebTools and how we use it.

Log Files

NodnWebTools follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

Privacy Policies

Third-party ad servers or ad networks uses technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on NodnWebTools, which are sent directly to users' browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs.

Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.
