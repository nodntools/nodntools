Free Global Stock Market Tracker

This Global Stock Market Tracker helps users search global stocks and view market charts, technical analysis, company profile information, and financial data in one browser-based dashboard. TradingView-Powered Stock Charts

The main chart area uses TradingView widgets to provide interactive market charts. Users can search common tickers such as AAPL, TSLA, NVDA, MSFT, AMZN, GOOGL, META, or use full exchange symbols like NASDAQ:AAPL, TSX:SHOP, and KRX:005930.

Better Than Browser Scraping
