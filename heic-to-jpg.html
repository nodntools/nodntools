What Is a HEIC to JPG Converter?

HEIC stands for High Efficiency Image Container and is the image format commonly used by Apple devices including iPhone and iPad. While HEIC files provide excellent image quality with smaller file sizes, they are not always supported by websites, software applications, printers, or older devices.

A HEIC to JPG converter transforms these files into the universally recognized JPG format. JPG files work almost everywhere, making them ideal for sharing, uploading, editing, and archiving photographs.
