Tips for Better Slideshow Results

For the best experience, use images with similar orientation and quality. Landscape photos usually work well with a 16:9 preview, while square or portrait images may leave empty space around the image so the full photo remains visible. Choose shorter slide durations for fast social-style presentations and longer durations for storytelling, memorial slideshows, wedding displays, or detailed photo galleries.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tool upload my photos to a server?

No. The slideshow is created in your browser. Your selected images are read locally by the page.

What file does the download button create?

The download button creates a standalone HTML slideshow file that includes your selected images and slideshow settings.

Can I make an MP4 video with this version?

This version focuses on slideshow preview and HTML slideshow download. MP4 video export requires more advanced browser encoding technology.

How many images can I use?

The practical limit depends on your device memory and image file sizes. For smoother performance, use optimized images and avoid extremely large files.
