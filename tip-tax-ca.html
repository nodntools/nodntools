How this Canada tip and tax calculator works

This tool estimates a final payment by adding the selected province or territory sales tax and your chosen tip rate. For a simple restaurant or store estimate, enter the amount before tax, choose the province, and select whether the tip should be calculated on the pre-tax amount or the post-tax amount.

Tax rate data included

The calculator includes GST/HST and provincial sales tax rates for Alberta, British Columbia, Manitoba, New Brunswick, Newfoundland and Labrador, Northwest Territories, Nova Scotia, Nunavut, Ontario, Prince Edward Island, Quebec, Saskatchewan, and Yukon.

Disclaimer: This calculator is for quick personal estimates only. Some items, food categories, liquor, delivery fees, exemptions, and local rules can be treated differently. Confirm the actual tax treatment on your receipt or with the business.
