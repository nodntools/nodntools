Free Real-time Currency Converter for USD, CAD, KRW, EUR and More

This real-time currency converter helps users quickly estimate the value of one currency against another using live exchange rate data. It is designed for common everyday use cases such as checking USD to CAD, CAD to USD, USD to KRW, CAD to KRW, EUR to CAD, JPY to USD, and other popular international currency pairs.

The calculator is especially useful for travellers, online shoppers, freelancers, students, immigrants, and anyone who needs a quick foreign exchange estimate before making a payment, sending money overseas, comparing prices, or reviewing credit card transactions. Because the tool runs directly in the browser, users can enter an amount, change the base currency, switch the target currency, or tap the swap button without reloading the page.

Exchange rates shown here are market reference rates from a public currency data API. Banks, credit card companies, money transfer services, airport exchange booths, and cash exchange stores may apply different rates, spreads, commissions, service fees, or foreign transaction fees. For example, the rate shown for 1 USD to CAD may not be exactly the same rate applied by your bank when you make an international card purchase.

How to use this converter

Enter an amount, choose the source currency, choose the target currency, and the converted value will update automatically using the latest loaded rate.

Popular currency pairs

Common searches include USD to CAD, CAD to USD, CAD to KRW, USD to KRW, EUR to USD, JPY to CAD, and GBP to CAD.

Why rates may differ

Actual payment rates may include bank spreads, card network rates, remittance fees, conversion fees, or local exchange office margins.
